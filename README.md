# 🛡️ CyberArk Defender – Access Certification Journey 🚀  
**Your 4-Week Masterplan to Ace the Exam**

> 🎓 **Goal**: Become CyberArk Defender – Access Certified  
> 📅 **Duration**: 4 Weeks  
> ✅ **Approach**: Learn + Practice + Review + Mock  
> 🔥 **Tools**: CyberArk Docs, Training Portal, YouTube, Practice Labs

---

## 📅 Weekly Planner Overview

| Week | Focus Area                                 | Goal                                                    |
|------|--------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| 1    | Identity & User Management                 | Build your CyberArk Identity foundation                  |
| 2    | SSO, Policies & MFA                        | Master Access Control, Authentication & App Configs      |
| 3    | Device Trust, Endpoints & LCM              | Learn provisioning and endpoint lifecycle management     |
| 4    | Revision + Real Scenarios + Mock Test      | Final review, troubleshoot real-world cases, exam prep   |

---

## 🧭 Weekly Breakdown

---

### 🗓️ Week 1: Build Your Identity IQ

**🔍 Topics to Master:**
- CyberArk Admin Portal Overview
- Roles & User Assignment
- Dashboards, Reports & Connectors
- User Portal Access
- Troubleshooting Basic User Issues

**🧠 Focus:**
- Hands-on with *User onboarding*
- Watch official videos on *CyberArk Identity Admin*

**🧰 Tools:**
- Admin Portal (Practice adding users)
- [CyberArk Identity Docs](https://docs.cyberark.com/ProductDoc/OnlineHelp/Idaptive/Latest/en/Content/Resources/_TopNav/cc_Home.htm)

**📝 Outcome:**
- Confident in user/role management and dashboards

---

### 🗓️ Week 2: Dive into SSO & Adaptive Policies

**🔐 Topics to Master:**
- App Gateway & Application Deployment
- Adding Apps: Office 365, Salesforce, Custom Apps
- Protocols: SAML, WS-Fed, OIDC
- Policies: Authentication, Endpoint, App
- IP Blocking & Policy Debugging

**🧠 Focus:**
- Practice App deployment and login flows (IDP vs SP)
- Create and troubleshoot access policies

**🛠️ Tools:**
- CyberArk App Catalog + Admin Portal  
- [CyberArk eLearning Portal](https://training.cyberark.com/category/idaptive-elearning)

**📝 Outcome:**
- Deploy, secure, and troubleshoot SSO apps with confidence

---

### 🗓️ Week 3: Protect Endpoints & Master LCM

**💻 Topics to Master:**
- MFA Fundamentals & Risk Analysis
- Authenticator Setup & Device Enrollment
- UBA + MFA Filters + Risk Reports
- Device Trust Enrollment (Windows)
- LCM Use Cases: Inbound & Outbound Provisioning

**🧠 Focus:**
- Setup Adaptive MFA with low prompt frequency
- Automate user provisioning with sample data

**📊 Tools:**
- CyberArk Authenticator Desktop & Mobile
- Windows VM for Device Trust
- HCM simulation (for LCM practice)

**📝 Outcome:**
- Confident in securing and automating identity lifecycle

---

### 🗓️ Week 4: Final Sprint & Real-World Confidence

**🏁 Activities:**
- Revise All 6 Domains
- Mock Scenarios:
  - "User can't access app – what’s wrong?"
  - "Provisioning failed – trace the flow"
- Practice Risk Reports + UBA Scenarios

**🧪 Mock Test:**
- Use sample questions or quiz yourself
- Simulate exam conditions (90 mins, no notes)

**💡 Bonus:**
- Create a *One-Pager Troubleshooting Guide*
- Summarize key protocols (SAML, OIDC) in your words

**📝 Outcome:**
- 100% Exam Ready + Real-world use case clarity

---
